Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 22, 2025

Important Notice: Private Folder Value - Safe is a secure file management application. We highly value your privacy protection. All your files, folders, and personal data are stored securely and are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

The types of information we collect include:

  • Required Information:
    • Account Information: Apple ID (for subscription management only)
    • Device Information: device model, operating system version (for app functionality)
    • App Usage Data: feature usage frequency, performance data (for service improvement)
    • Crash Reports: technical data related to app crashes (for issue resolution)
  • File Management Data:
    • Files and Folders: documents, images, and files you store in the app
    • File Metadata: file names, creation dates, and organization structure
    • Secret Space Data: encrypted files stored in your private vault
    • iCloud Sync Data: files synchronized with your iCloud account (if enabled)
    • All file data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our file management services
  • Process your subscription payments (through Apple's payment system)
  • Synchronize your files across devices via iCloud (if enabled)
  • Protect your files with encryption in Secret Space
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Save your preferences and custom settings

3. Data Storage and Processing

Regarding data processing, please note:

  • All your files are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account (if sync is enabled)
  • We do not access, view, or share your files without your explicit consent
  • Secret Space files are encrypted with your password and cannot be accessed by anyone else
  • All file data is deleted upon app uninstallation
  • Analytics data uses Apple's privacy-preserving analytics service
  • No file content is transmitted to our servers

4. Secret Space Security

Our Secret Space feature provides enhanced security:

  • We do not store or have access to your Secret Space password
  • If you forget your password, we cannot recover your Secret Space files
  • Secret Space files are hidden from regular file browsing
  • Access is only possible through the search box with the correct password

5. iCloud Integration

When you enable iCloud sync:

  • Files are synchronized using Apple's iCloud service
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • Sync is governed by Apple's privacy policy
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

6. Subscription Services

Regarding subscriptions:

  • Payment will be charged to your iTunes account
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings → iTunes & App Store → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription

7. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Local data encryption storage
  • Secure file processing mechanisms
  • Password-protected Secret Space with strong encryption
  • Regular security reviews
  • Strict internal access controls

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us for deletion.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your files and data at any time
  • Control iCloud sync settings
  • Manage Secret Space access
  • Opt-out of analytics data collection

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last Updated" date on this page. Your continued use of our services after such changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. App Store Data Usage and Privacy

Our application complies with Apple's App Store privacy label requirements. You can view detailed data collection and usage information on our App Store page.
