Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

Important Notice: Stardel is an AI-powered photo gallery app based on local processing technology. We highly value your privacy, and all photo processing is performed locally on your device without cloud uploading.

1. Information We Collect

The types of information we collect include:

  • Required Information:
    • Device information: device model, OS version, device identifiers (for app functionality)
    • App usage data: feature usage frequency, performance data (for service improvement)
    • Crash reports: technical data related to app crashes (for bug fixing)
  • Permissions:
    • Photo library access: for gallery management features
    • Face recognition: for facial detection features (local processing only)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your subscription payments (via Apple's payment system)
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests

3. Data Storage and Processing

Regarding data processing:

  • All photo-related AI processing is performed locally on your device
  • We do not upload, store, or process your photos
  • Facial recognition data is stored and used only on your device
  • Analytics data uses Apple's privacy-preserving analytics service

4. Subscription Services

Regarding subscriptions:

  • Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings → iTunes & App Store → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription

5. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Local data encryption
  • Secure processing mechanisms
  • Regular security reviews
  • Strict internal access controls

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that we may have collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove the information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Policy Updates

Our application is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We take user privacy protection seriously, especially the privacy and safety of children. Our application runs entirely locally and does not collect or upload any personal information to servers, including information about child users.

9. App Store Data Usage and Privacy

Our app complies with Apple's App Store privacy labels requirements. You can view detailed data collection and usage information on our App Store page.
