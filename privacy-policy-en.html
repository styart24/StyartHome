Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2024

Important Notice: Styart Photos is an intelligent photo album application based on local AI technology. We highly value your privacy protection. All photo and facial feature processing is performed locally on your device and will not be uploaded to the cloud. We do not collect, upload, or share any of your photos or facial data.

1. Information We Collect

The types of information we collect include:

  • Required Information:
    • Device Information: device model, operating system version, device identifier (for app functionality)
    • App Usage Data: feature usage frequency, performance data (for service improvement)
    • Crash Reports: technical data related to app crashes (for issue resolution)
  • Photo and Facial Data Processing:
    • Photo Library Access: for providing photo management features
    • Facial Feature Data: for photo smart categorization and quick search
      • Analysis of facial features in device photos
      • Feature extraction for similar face grouping
      • All data stored locally on device only
      • No identity recognition, only similarity analysis

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your subscription payments (through Apple's payment system)
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Enable photo smart categorization and quick search (based on local facial feature analysis)

3. Data Storage and Processing

Regarding data processing, please note:

  • All photo-related AI processing is performed locally on device
  • Facial feature data is stored and used locally only:
    • Used for photo smart categorization and quick search
    • Never uploaded to cloud or shared with third parties
    • All related data automatically deleted upon app uninstallation
    • No identity recognition performed
  • Analytics data uses Apple's privacy-preserving analytics service

4. Subscription Services

Regarding subscriptions:

  • Payment will be charged to your iTunes account
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device's Settings → iTunes & App Store → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Any unused portion of a free trial period will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription

5. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Local data encryption storage
  • Secure data processing mechanisms
  • Regular security reviews
  • Strict internal access controls

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We highly value user privacy protection, especially the privacy security of children. Our application operates entirely locally and does not collect or upload any user personal information to servers, including information about child users.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access and export your data
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last Updated" date on this page. Your continued use of our services after such changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. App Store Data Usage and Privacy

Our application complies with Apple's App Store privacy label requirements. You can view detailed data collection and usage information on our App Store page. Special note: Our facial feature processing is performed entirely locally and we do not collect or upload any related data.
